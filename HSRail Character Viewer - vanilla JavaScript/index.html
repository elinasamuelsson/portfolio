Honkai Star Rail character viewer

Welcome to my character viewer page where you will be able to look through my Honkai Star Rail roster and the builds (or lack thereof) of each character. The webpage displays my main team, secondary team, and a list of all characters in my roster.

Each character image will be clickable and will open a pop-up with additional information on the character's lightcone and relics setup, as well as stats and talent progress.

The roster list will also be searchable.
